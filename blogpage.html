What does it mean to have a cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a digital currency, which is an alternative form of payment created using blockchain.

But what is a Blockchain? The Wikipedia says,

A blockchain is a type of distributed ledger technology (DLT) that consists of growing list of records, called blocks, that are securely linked together using cryptography.

Well this doesn't help much, at least for a beginner. Let's take a bit more constructive approach towards the topic.

Why use a ledger?

Say you have a group of few friends and you exchange money on various occasions throughout the month. It might be inconvenient to keep cash always to exchange, instead you create a website like a ledger where you keep entries like, Alice giver Bob 100 Rs. Bob gives Jim 50 Rs. And so on... Now this ledger should be, * Public, so that during each transaction one can add lines to it * To confirm the transactions it should contain the signature of the payer.
